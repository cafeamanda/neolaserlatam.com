PROCTOLOGY

Proctology conditions such as hemorrhoids and fistula-in-ano are very prevalent in western societies.

Some treatment options exist with their advantages and disadvantages.

Neolaser introduces ALFA™ and ELITE™ for laser treatment of fistula-in-ano and grade 3-4 hemorrhoids, respectively. These microsurgical tools enable safe, sphincter saving and almost pain free procedure for patients and surgeons.

 THE ALFA™ PROCEDURE

During an ALFA™ procedure laser energy is delivered, via a radial emitting optical fiber, into the anal fistula tract and is used to thermally ablate and close off the abnormal pathway. The laser energy induces destruction of the fistula epithelium and simultaneous obliteration of the remaining fistula tract by a shrinkage effect.

 THE ELITE™ PROCEDURE

In the ELITE™ procedure laser energy is delivered via a designated optical fiber into the hemorrhoidal pile and is used to thermally close off the veins nourishing the abnormal growth. The laser energy induces destruction of the venous epithelium and simultaneous obliteration of the hemorrhoidal pile by a shrinkage effect.
