ENDO-VASCULAR

El NeoV es indicado para Ablación Endovenosa Láser (EVLA), en el tratamiento de venas con várices. La enfermedad predomina significativamente en la población en general. Hasta 55% de las mujeres y 45% de los hombres sufren de molestias venosas. De ello, hasta 25% de las mujeres y 15% de los hombres tendrán várices visibles.

En los métodos de tratamiento quirúrgico están inclusos cirugía abierta, stripping y técnicas mínimamente invasivas. La Ablación Endovenosa Láser (EVLA) proporciona la herramienta ideal para el tratamiento de venas en un método mínimamente invasivo, con tasas de complicación reducida y alta eficiencia.

Aplicando a la energía del láser, el interior de la pared del vaso queda irreversiblemente destrozado y, de esa forma, elimina la vena disfuncional. El vaso es reabsorbido en los meses siguientes.

La mayoría de los estudios ha demostrado recuperación más rápida y menos complicaciones utilizando técnicas de tratamiento mínimamente invasivas. La introducción reciente de energía 1470nm, con absorción más elevada en el agua causando menos daños colaterales térmicos, ha mejorado la técnica aún más, permitiendo la utilización segura, rápida y precisa de energía para coagular el vaso con mínimo impacto sobre las estructuras vecinas.

 THE EVLA PROCEDURE

The Endovenous Laser Ablation procedure utilizes ultrasound guidance and tumescent anesthesia to enable treatment. A puncture is performed allowing percutaneous entry and access up to the saphenofemoral junction. A guide wire, visible under ultrasound, is advanced to the treatment area, followed by the introducer. The guide wire is removed and replaced with the optical fiber for energy delivery.

Energy is delivered while pulling back the fiber and introducer enabling deposition of energy along the entire length of the vein. The ease of use and minimally invasive nature of the apparatus allows rapid completion of the treatment; patients can return to normal activities immediately post treatment.

Enabling rapid treatment with optimal setup and portability, the neoV laser system will provide fast return on investment, allowing you to expand your practice.

Optimal Energy Delivery Fibers

We offer 2 different versions of fibers.

All fibers are provided with full procedural kits with the latest advances in endovascular technology.

Corona 360 Fiber Technology

Neolaser's Corona 360 fiber technology provides you with the efficiency of circular emission, ensuring deposition of energy directly on the vessel wall.

By aiming light at 60 degrees relative to the fiber axis, coagulation is guaranteed during pullback of the fiber.

The tip of the fiber is comprised of an extra smooth glass capillary, directly connected to a marked smooth jacket, allowing easy direct insertion into the vein.

The fiber utilizes a simple procedure kit with a short introducer, reducing steps and procedure time.

The introducer is compatible with both CORONA 360 and CORONA 360 Slim and matches the CORONA fiber markings for safety and efficacy of the procedure.

 THE NEOV 1470

Your Choice of Wavelength

Laser interaction with tissue depends on the wavelength of choice. The neoV 1470 diode laser offers a novel wavelength with superior performance for ablation of diseased veins – 1470nm. In contrast to previous generations of laser therapy with deep tissue penetration, the 1470nm wavelength is highly absorbed by water in tissue. As a result heat generation is highly localized and application is safe and precise.

Safe and Homogeneous Energy Deposition

The neoV laser provides the user with a synchronization signal which relates to the desired dosage, specified in J per cm, enabling the user to carefully and safely match pullback speed to the desired dosage.

The neoV laser comes equipped with predefined programs and may be easily programmed by the user for specific dosages and pullbacks speeds which may relate to vein diameter and the type of energy used.

This unique feature provides for safe and homogeneous deposition of energy along the vein wall.
